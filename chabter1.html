Chapter 1: Introduction to Chemical Compounds

Chemical compounds are substances composed of two or more different types of elements, chemically bonded together in fixed proportions. They have unique properties that distinguish them from their constituent elements.

Types of Chemical Compounds

There are various types of chemical compounds, including:

  • Ionic Compounds: Formed from the electrostatic attraction between positively and negatively charged ions.
  • Covalent Compounds: Formed when two atoms share one or more pairs of electrons.
  • Metallic Compounds: Consist of metal atoms bonded together by a 'sea' of delocalized electrons.

Understanding these compounds is crucial for the study of chemistry and many related fields.
